What is the apothem of polygon?
The apothem is a line from center point of side of a regular polygon to the midpoint of the regular polygon.
How to calculate apothem of polygon?
To calculate apothem of polygon you should use the following formula:
apothem of polygon = length of any side of polygon / (2 * tan(180 / number of sides of a polygon))
